# BrightPath Community Care

Welcome to BrightPath Community Care! We're a community-driven initiative dedicated to making a positive impact through clothing donations, food support, and educational programs. This repository contains the official website for our organization.

## About Us

BrightPath Community Care is committed to empowering individuals and families in our community. We believe in creating pathways to opportunity through charitable giving, resource sharing, and educational initiatives. Whether you're looking to donate, get involved, or learn more about our mission, you've come to the right place.

## What We Do

- **Clothing Donations** - Providing essential clothing to those in need
- **Food Support** - Distributing food assistance to vulnerable community members
- **Educational Programs** - Offering tutorials and resources to help people improve their skills and knowledge
- **Community Engagement** - Building connections and fostering community spirit

## Our Website

This website serves as a hub for our community to:
- Learn about our mission and values
- Browse our gallery of past initiatives
- Donate to support our cause
- Submit enquiries and get involved
- Stay updated on our latest news

## Explore Our Pages

- **Home** (`index.html`) - Welcome and overview of BrightPath Community Care
- **About** (`about.html`) - Learn our story, mission, and values
- **Gallery** (`gallery.html`) - See highlights from our past projects and initiatives
- **Get Involved** (`get-involved.html`) - Discover ways you can help and contribute
- **Donate** (`donate.html`) - Support our mission with a donation
- **Contact** (`contact.html`) - Reach out with questions or feedback
- **Enquiry** (`enquiry.html`) - Submit specific requests or inquiries

## Technologies Used

## Getting Started

To view the website locally:

1. Clone this repository:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/st10489217/BrightPath-Community-Care.git
   ```

2. Navigate to the project directory:
   ```bash
   cd BrightPath-Community-Care
   ```

3. Open `index.html` in your web browser to view the site
